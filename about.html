About Us

Special Reserve awakens the spirit of adventure by providing a luxurious journey to your destination where you get intrigued by the authentic sounds of the spectacular mother nature. To enjoy an unforgettable travel experience, book with us. Special Reserve is an affordable travel agency for exploring wonders of mother nature such as the spectacular habitat of rhinoceros in Nairobi National Park found within the green city in the sun, Nairobi, the capital city of Kenya. You will enjoy amazing places to visit such as:

Giraffe Center, a home of Rothschild giraffes where kissing a giraffe is the game. Bomas of Kenya, the homesteads, and traditional villages belonging to several Kenyan tribes. The Sheldrick Wildlife Trust, which serves as an orphanage for baby elephants. Nairobi National Museum, where collections of Kenya's history, artifacts, and contemporary art are displayed. Hiking along Ngong Forest.

TRAVELING WITH STANDARD GAUGE RAILWAY: We link you from Nairobi to Mombasa through S.G.R, as you explore various spectacular terrestrial ecosystems, the grasses with short scattered trees along Tsavo East National Park which is patrolled by some of the largest elephant herds in Kenya. The first-class standard gauge provides comfort and luxurious travel to Mombasa town. The booking of S.G.R is done by Special Reserve Limited. Special Reserve Limited provides the following:
